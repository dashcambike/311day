On March 11th, learn how to report mobility issues to 311, your city's non-emergency line.

National 311 Day is a movement to teach residents how to communicate with their city. Individuals and groups devoted to safer streets can host their own events. Find a walk or bike ride near you, or host your own!

On these walks, your host will guide you through problem areas, and show you how to make 311 reports for the hazards you encounter.
